---
title: AI for Everyone
description: An introductory AI column for non-technical readers — no code, no jargon, just everyday analogies that make AI make sense.
---

Your photos app recognizes the cat, your keyboard finishes your sentence, your speaker knows whether it'll rain tomorrow — AI has quietly woven itself into daily life, yet few explanations actually make it click. This series starts from the situations you run into every day and walks through how AI works, what it can do for you, and where its limits lie. Reading in chapter order is recommended.
